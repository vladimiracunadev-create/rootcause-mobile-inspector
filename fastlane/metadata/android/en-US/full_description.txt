RootCause Mobile Inspector is a forensic diagnostic sensor for Android: it
watches anomalous distortions of your phone's resources —memory, storage,
battery, network and the permission surface of your apps— and explains
with evidence when something behaves differently. Diagnosis first,
intervention second.

It is not an antivirus or a "cleaner": it does not remove malware or kill
apps (Android forbids that for a user app). It shows you signals with
their evidence and takes you to the system screen where you decide.

PRIVACY BY DESIGN
• No INTERNET permission: the app cannot send anything, even if it wanted
  to. Verifiable in the code.
• Zero telemetry, zero accounts, zero ads.
• Your evidence only leaves the phone if you share it.

WHAT IT OBSERVES
• Global traffic-light verdict with a local rule engine (memory, storage,
  battery temperature/health, risky apps, newly installed apps, outdated
  security patch, sustained rising load, root/jailbreak).
• Per-app permission surface audit and screen time (with the optional
  usage access).
• Storage volumes (SD card / USB).
• Bluetooth LE scan of nearby devices (opt-in).
• Local history with a trend chart and snapshot comparison.

REAL EVIDENCE
• Every snapshot is sealed with a SHA-256 hash chain: evidence is
  verifiable, not tamperable.
• Shareable forensic report and full backup/restore of your evidence.
• Background watch with a local notification if something turns critical
  or a spyware-like app is installed.

Free software (Apache 2.0). Mobile sibling of rootcause-windows-inspector.
